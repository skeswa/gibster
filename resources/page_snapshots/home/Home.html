    Would you like to request a Lockout (Full-Day Rental)?

    A Lockout is a full-day studio booking during our operating hours, offering uninterrupted access to your designated studio for the entire day. This rental option is ideal for events, rehearsals, or productions that require exclusive use of the space without interruption. Lockouts are available for our most advance scheduling and offer flexibility to accommodate your creative needs.

    *Lockout

    Would you like to request space for a Performance?

    This rental option provides at least 4 hours of access to one of our performance spaces, including use of the lighting board, sound system, and seating. Ideal for hosting a performance or a showing, this booking is designed to meet the needs of your event with technical amenities available, but without the full-day lockout.

    *Performance/Showing
